How It Works

Intelligent engagement in 5 strategic phases

1

Identification

System detects scam intent and identifies scam type through pattern matching and AI analysis.

2

Build Trust

AI persona establishes credibility and concern, making the scammer believe they're dealing with a real victim.

3

Extract Intel

Intelligently asks questions to extract account numbers, UPI IDs, contact information, and sensitive details.

4

Delay & Probe

Introduces obstacles and hesitations to buy time for analysis while probing for inconsistencies in scammer's story.

5

Safe Exit

Gracefully exits conversation while suggesting reporting to authorities. All intelligence extracted and logged.
