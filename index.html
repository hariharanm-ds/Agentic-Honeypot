How to Use

1. Setup

Select a victim persona from the dropdown and click "Create Conversation" to start a new session. The system will assign a unique conversation ID.

2. Interact

Enter a message as if you're a scammer. The AI victim will respond with contextually appropriate replies based on the conversation phase.

3. Analyze

View real-time scam detection results, extracted intelligence (UPI, phone, links, accounts, emails), and conversation metrics.

4. Export

Export the complete conversation with all extracted intelligence as JSON for law enforcement or further analysis.
